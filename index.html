Moraband Chess Engine

Moraband is a UCI-compatible chess engine written in C++, featuring modern search and evaluation techniques, with a legal move generator measuring over 200,000,000 nodes/second (moves/second). Developing my own chess engine was a great way to combine my passion of chess with my love of computer programming!

Code: github.com/bistromath25/moraband

Demo: lichess.org/@/morabandbot

imgdb

Built with node js (taking advantage of the multer package) and hosted on replit, imgdb provides a straight-forward solution to storing your images online. Simply upload an image, and visit the resulting link whenever you wish to retrieve it.

Code: github.com/bistromath25/imgdb

Demo: imgdb.bistromath25.repl.co
