Moraband Chess Engine

Moraband is a UCI-compatible chess engine written in C++, featuring modern search and evaluation techniques, with a move generator measuring over 160,000,000 nodes/second (moves/second) on a single thread.

imgdb

Built with node js (taking advantage of the multer package), imgdb provides a straight-forward solution to storing your images online. Simply upload an image, and visit the resulting link whenever you wish to retrieve it and share with friends!
